Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does carpet installation cost on average?

The carpet installation cost typically depends on the square footage, the type of carpet fiber selected, and the quality of the padding. We provide detailed estimates that include both materials and labor, ensuring there are no hidden fees during your carpet replacement.

2. Can you perform hardwood floor refinishing without sanding?

In some cases, we offer refinishing hardwood floors without sanding through a process known as screening and recoating. However, for deep scratches or color changes, a full hardwood floor sanding and floor refinishing service is usually recommended for the best results.

3. How long does it take for carpets to dry after a professional cleaning?

Most carpets in #State will be fully dry within 4 to 8 hours. We use high-powered extraction and air movers to speed up the process so you can get back to your normal routine quickly.

4. Do you offer commercial flooring services for large businesses?

Yes, we provide comprehensive commercial flooring solutions, including vct flooring, industrial epoxy flooring, and commercial carpet cleaning. We are equipped to handle large-scale projects for retail spaces, warehouses, and office buildings nationwide.

5. Is vinyl plank flooring installation a good choice for kitchens?

Absolutely. Vinyl plank flooring installation is one of our most popular services because LVP (Luxury Vinyl Plank) is 100% waterproof and highly scratch-resistant, making it the perfect flooring and installation choice for kitchens, bathrooms, and basements.

6. What is the benefit of an epoxy floor for a garage?

An epoxy garage floor provides a seamless, non-porous surface that is resistant to oil spills, chemicals, and impact. Our garage floor coatings are much more durable than standard concrete paint and significantly increase the longevity of your garage floor.
